# Complexity: Simple

> **Council Reviews:** 1
> **Human Gates:** 0-1
> **Best for:** Short, single-voice content

---

## Configuration

```yaml
complexity: simple

reviews:
  council_reviews: 1
  council_mode: quick
  review_points: [after_production]

human_interaction:
  human_gates: 0-1
  gates: [final_approval]  # Optional

skipped_agents:
  - trend-analyst
  - scene-director
  - art-director
  - prompt-engineer
  - image-producer
  - video-producer
  - thumbnail-designer

iterations:
  revision_rounds: 1
  extra_iterations: false
```

---

## When to Use

- Short stories (under 5 minutes)
- Single voice narrator
- No visual content
- Quick turnaround needed
- Low complexity narrative

---

## Estimated Time

1-3 hours
