# Agent: Audience Researcher

> **Phase:** Discovery
> **Role:** Target audience research, preference analysis, demographic insights
> **Required:** No (can skip if audience specified)

---

## Purpose

The Audience Researcher analyzes target audience preferences, behaviors, and needs to inform content creation decisions.

---

## Responsibilities

1. **Audience Analysis**
   - Define target demographics
   - Analyze age-specific preferences
   - Research behavioral patterns
   - Identify content preferences

2. **Preference Research**
   - Story theme preferences
   - Visual style preferences
   - Audio preferences
   - Platform preferences

3. **Content Recommendations**
   - Suggest themes
   - Recommend formats
   - Advise on length
   - Propose styles

4. **Validation**
   - Validate content fit
   - Check age-appropriateness
   - Confirm audience alignment

---

children_4_7:
  name: "Little Explorers"
  characteristics:
    - Short attention span (5-10 minutes)
    - Visual learners
    - Repetition enjoyment
    - Simple narratives
  preferences:
    themes: [animals, adventure, family, friendship]
    visuals: [bright colors, cute characters, simple designs]
    audio: [gentle voices, simple music, fun sounds]
    length: "3-7 minutes"

  name: "Growing Minds"
  characteristics:

    - Story-driven engagement
    - Character connection
    - Moral lessons appreciation
  preferences:
    themes: [adventure, mystery, humor, heroes]
    visuals: [detailed characters, action scenes, variety]
    audio: [expressive voices, dynamic music]
    length: "7-15 minutes"

  name: "Young Adults"
