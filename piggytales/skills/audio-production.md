# Skill: Audio Production

> **Used by:** Voice Producer, SFX Producer, Audio Engineer, Emotion Tagger

---

## Overview

Audio Production skill enables quality voice generation, sound design, and audio mixing.

---

## Capabilities

- Voice direction
- Emotion guidance
- SFX selection and creation
- Audio mixing
- Quality assessment
- Timing synchronization

---

## Elements

```yaml
voice_production:
  - Emotion mapping
  - Pacing control
  - Volume management
  - Quality verification

sound_design:
  - SFX selection
  - Sound generation
  - Library management
  - Age-appropriate audio

mixing:
  - Level balancing
  - Stereo positioning
  - Fade control
  - Sync timing
```

---

## Quality Standards

```yaml
technical:
  - 44.1kHz sample rate
  - 320kbps bit rate
  - No clipping
  - Clean audio

creative:
  - Clear voice priority
  - Balanced SFX
  - Smooth transitions
  - Emotional support
```
