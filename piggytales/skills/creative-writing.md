# Skill: Creative Writing

> **Used by:** Script Writer, Emotion Tagger, Council agents

---

## Overview

Creative Writing skill enables compelling storytelling, dialogue creation, and narrative development.

---

## Capabilities

- Story structure development
- Character voice creation
- Age-appropriate dialogue
- Hook writing
- Emotional narrative
- Pacing management

---

## Techniques

```yaml
storytelling:
  - Three-act structure
  - Hero's journey adaptation
  - Character arcs
  - Conflict resolution

hooks:
  - Question hooks
  - Action hooks
  - Mystery hooks
  - Emotional hooks

dialogue:
  - Natural conversation
  - Age-appropriate language
  - Character-specific voice
  - Emotional expression
```

---

## Age Adaptations

```yaml
children_4_7:
  - Simple vocabulary
  - Short sentences
  - Concrete concepts
  - Repetition

children_8_12:
  - Moderate complexity
  - More nuance
  - Some abstract concepts

teenagers:
  - Complex narratives
  - Sophisticated themes
  - Contemporary language
```
