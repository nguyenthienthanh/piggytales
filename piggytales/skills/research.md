# Skill: Research

> **Used by:** Content Scout, Trend Analyst, Legal Checker, Audience Researcher

---

## Overview

Research skill enables agents to discover, analyze, and document information from various sources.

---

## Capabilities

- Public domain source research
- Copyright status verification
- Trend analysis
- Audience behavior research
- Competitor analysis
- Platform algorithm research

---

## Methods

```yaml
source_research:
  - Public domain databases
  - Library archives
  - Folk tale collections
  - Historical records

trend_research:
  - Platform analytics
  - Popular content analysis
  - Hashtag tracking
  - Viral pattern identification

legal_research:
  - Copyright databases
  - Publication records
  - License verification
  - Policy documentation
```

---

## Quality Standards

- Verify all sources
- Document citations
- Cross-reference information
- Note limitations
- Flag uncertainties
